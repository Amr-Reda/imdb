Andrew Stanton

American film director

Andrew Stanton is an American film director, screenwriter, producer and voice actor based at Pixar, which he joined in 1990. His film work includes writing and directing Pixar's A Bug's Life, Finding Nemo and its sequel Finding Dory, WALL-E, and the live-action film, Disney's John Carter. Wikipedia
